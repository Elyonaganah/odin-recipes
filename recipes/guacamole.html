Guacamole

Description

Indulge in the vibrant flavors of our freshly made guacamole, served to perfection on a rustic dish. Our guacamole recipe brings together the finest avocados, ripe tomatoes, zesty lime juice, and aromatic cilantro, creating a harmonious blend of textures and tastes. Garnished with a sprinkle of diced onions and a dash of chili flakes for an extra kick, this delightful dish promises a mouthwatering experience with every bite.

Whether paired with crispy tortilla chips or as a flavorful accompaniment to your favorite Mexican dishes, our guacamole is sure to be the star of your table. Get ready to dip, savor, and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Ripe Avocados
  • Fresh Lime Juice
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Finely Chopped Red Onion
  • Minced Garlic
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Ground cumin

Steps

  1. Step 1: Prepare the avocados
      Cut the avocados in half lengthwise and remove the pits. Scoop the avocado flesh into a mixing bowl using a spoon.
  2. Step 2: Mash the avocados
      Use a fork or potato masher to mash the avocado until desired consistency is reached. Some prefer it chunky, while others like it smoother.
  3. Step 3: Add lime juice
      Squeeze fresh lime juice over the mashed avocados. Start with one lime and adjust to taste.
  4. Step 4: Mix in diced tomatoes
      Add the diced tomatoes to the mashed avocado. You can use Roma tomatoes or any other variety you prefer.
  5. Step 5: Add finely chopped red onion
      Mix in the finely chopped red onion for a sharp flavor and crunchy texture.
  6. Step 6: Stir in minced garlic
      Add the minced garlic to the mixture for a hint of pungent flavor. Adjust the amount to taste.
  7. Step 7: Season with salt, pepper, and ground cumin
      Sprinkle salt, pepper, and ground cumin over the mixture. Start with a pinch of each and adjust according to taste preferences.
  8. Step 8: Mix well
      Gently mix all the ingredients together until well combined. Be careful not to overmix, especially if you prefer a chunkier guacamole.
  9. Step 9: Taste and adjust seasoning
      Taste the guacamole and adjust the seasoning as needed. You may need to add more lime juice, salt, pepper, or any other ingredient to balance the flavors.
  10. Step 10: Serve and enjoy
      Transfer the guacamole to a serving bowl and garnish with additional chopped cilantro or a slice of lime if desired. Serve immediately with tortilla chips or as a topping for tacos, nachos, or any of your favorite dishes.
  11. Step 11: Optional - Chill
      If you prefer your guacamole chilled, cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for about 30 minutes before serving. This allows the flavors to meld together even more.

Enjoy your meal!
